Speghetti

Description

This modified rendition of the spaghetti dish is a mouth watering, stomach filling and tastebud satifying. On top of all that goodness, there's more...IT'S QUICK AND EASY TO MAKE!!


Ingredients

  • 1 pound of spaghetti
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 1 (15 ounce) can of black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans
  • 1 cup of sweet corn kernels
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 dash of hot pepper sauce
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of grated Parmeasan cheese

Steps

  1. In a large skillet heat cook the onion in the olive oil until tender. Add the spaghetti sauce, black beans, kidney beans, corn and mix well. Stir in the cumin, red pepper sauce, salt and ground black pepper. simmer for 15 to 20 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, in a large pot with boiling salted water cook spagherri until all done. Drain.
  3. Toss pasta with spaghetti sauce. Serve with freshly grated Parmesan cheese.
